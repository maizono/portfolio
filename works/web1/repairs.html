Repairs

Need something to be repaired? Follow these steps for the smoothest experience possible!

  • Step 1: Give us a call!
    • Tell our experts all about your technical issues. Specific information, such as the brand of your device, will help us find a solution faster!
  • Step 2: Diagnosis
    • Once we analyze your issues, we will give you a diagnosis. If possible, we will give you at-home steps to fix it absolutely free!
  • Step 3: Preparing your Device
    • Sometimes, there's just no way to fix a problem at home, and our experts here at Kid Tech must deal with it hands-on.
      • Tell the expert on the phone that you would like to send a device in for repair.
      • Carefully package your device.
      • Check our contact page to find our mailing address.
      • Send your device to us.
      • Once we receive your device, we will send you a confirmation e-mail for payment and begin working on it.
      • When the device has been successfully repaired and payment has been received, we will send it back to you. Shipping could take 5-8 business days.
  • Step 4: Let us know how we did!
    • Your feedback is very important to us! Once you receive your device back from us, you will receive an optional e-mail to give us feedback.
